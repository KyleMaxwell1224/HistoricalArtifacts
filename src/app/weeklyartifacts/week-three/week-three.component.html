For my reflection this week, I chose to do 'Slow Looking'. I did this same activity in week one, but I used the 'Categories to guide the eye' method. This time, I will use the 'Open Inventories' method. The image is a fold out engraving depicting a curiosity cabinet from Ferrante Imperato’s Dell’Historia Naturale. The instructions are as follow:

  1. 10 x 3: Look at an image or object for at least 1 minute. Let your eyes wander.
  2. List 10 words or phrases about anything you notice.
  3. Repeat steps a and b, adding 10 more words or phrases to your list.
  4. Repeat steps a and b again, adding 10 more words or phrases to your list.
